Facts and figures

  • There are nearly 12 million (11,989,322) people aged 65 and above in the UK of which: 5.4 million people are aged 75+, 1.6 million are aged 85+, Over 500,000 people are 90+ (579,776), 14,430 are centenarians
  • Centre for Better Ageing report states that a fifth of people in this age group have seen their physical health deteriorate during the lockdown period, and over a third say their mental health has worsened
  • People who are 80 or older were 70X more likely to die than those under 40

Needs to consider in your designs

  • I need to be informed about the process and purpose of testing so that I can feel prepared for it
  • I need an option to test from the safety of my home with the support of another person or on the phone
  • I need a way of getting my test delivered to my home without an email or phone
  • I need the testing experience to be connected to my GP and/or pharmacist
  • I need reliable guidance about the testing process and about shielding instructions
  • I need a way of getting a test and my results by phone call
