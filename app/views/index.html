Inclusive design tool

We've divided groups uncovered in research which make people vulnerable or high risk into four categories.

In real life, people may present a combination of one or more characteristics from each of these factors, resulting in complex and varied needs.

The needs and attitudes of people are constantly changing. Being able to flexibly layer multiple factors from each category gives a more dynamic understanding of people's needs and behaviours.
